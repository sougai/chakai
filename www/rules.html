茶道

「一期一会」

『ichi-go-ichi-e』 - One time, one meeting

Principles:

  1. Harmony 「和」 - Everything is connected
  2. Respect 「敬」 - Open your heart
  3. Purity 「清」 - Clear the dust of the world
  4. Tranquility 「寂」 -

Host:

  1. Make a delicious bowl of tea
  2. Lay the charcoal so that it heats the water
  3. Arrange the flowers as they are in the field
  4. In summer, suggest coolness; in winter, warmth
  5. Do everything ahead of time
  6. Prepare for rain
  7. Give those with whom you find yourself every consideration

Guest:

  1. Take it easy
  2. What one has is all one needs
  3. Keep minority spirit
